Lasagna

Description

Ingredients:

  • Pasta: Main ingredient for this dish is the lasagna pasta
  • Meat: It is recommended to use half part lean beef and half part pork
  • Onion: A diced onion is cooked until it is translucent
  • Canned tomatoes: You will need a can of tomato sauce and a can of crushed tomatoes
  • Sugar: A dash of sugar balances out all of the acidity from the tomatoes
  • Spices and seasonings: Dried basil, dried oregano, salt and black pepper will liven your dish
  • Cheese: The cheese layer is made up of cottage cheese, Parmesan, and shredded mozzarella

Steps:

  1. Cooking the noodles: Boil the lasagna pasta until it is al dante
  2. Cooking the meat: Cook the meat in a skillet until it is brown. Add the onion and continue cooking until it is translucent. Stri in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, a pinch of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  3. Prepping the cheese: Mix in the cheeses, a pinch of salt, and a pinch of pepper in a separate bowl
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients (starting with sauce and ending with the cheese mix) until the lasagna is assembled
  5. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the pregeated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and bake until the top is golden brown
