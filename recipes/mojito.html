Mojito

Description

Ingredients:

  • Rum:It is best to use a light rum for this drink
  • Mint:A key component for this drink is fresh mint
  • Lime:Use freshly squeezed limes for best results
  • Sugar:Use simple syrup as it blends well into the drink
  • Club Soda:Top off the drink with club soda for a refreshing drink

Steps:

  1. Step 1:Place mint leaves and a 2 lime wedges into a sturdy glass, then muddle
  2. Step 2:Add 2 more lime wedges and 1 ounce of simple syrup, muddle again
  3. Step 3:Fill glass with ice
  4. Step 4:Add 2 ounces of light rum and fill glass with club soda
  5. Step 5:Stir and enjoy!
